about me

This beautiful 2 story showroom in Canterbury, Victoria is home to Yen Concept. Showcasing a range of high quality furniture, art and decor pieces. The showroom is a series of styled rooms to help you visualise the pieces within your own home. Established 20 years ago Yen Concept has seen trends come and go and enjoys the challenge of changing decors and colour schemes. Although we are know for the "YEN" look which is gracing homes throughout Melbourne we love the challenge of all interiors
